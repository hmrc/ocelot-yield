Stencil completion

Note:

Once all of the required details have been entered on the stencil the yield will be automatically calculated.

Stencil Completion
  • insert name and NINO/UTR of the claimant
  • select 'relevant drop down selection
  • complete all relevant fields within the stencil ensuring that the correct tax rate is used. Where the tax year straddles tax rates always select the lower rate.
  • select 'copy note', this will enable you to paste the details of the claim in to PAYE notes - the button will turn green to confirm
  • select submit - to confirm submission the button will show 'successfully submitted'
Stencils submitted incorrectly

Where information has been submitted incorrectly on the PT Operations Yield Collector stencil action will be required to correct this information

Incorrect personal details submitted on stencil
  • complete a further stencil ensuring that the same NINO/UTR is used
  • enter zero in all of the monetary fields
  • submit - this will reverse the original incorrect entry to nil
  • submit a further stencil where required with the correct information
Incorrect monetary values submitted on stencil
  • Submit a further stencil with the correct information ensuring that the same NINO/UTR is used. This will then disregard the original submission
